Game Time!!!!

Welcome to the math game, where you will have practice in counting, addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division,
while playing a fun game!

Goal of the game: To pass all three levels and reach the crown

Rules:
~ There are three levels. The first level consists of counting problems. The second level consists of addition/subtraction problems. The third level consists of multiplication/division problems.
~ The first level has two problems, while the second and third has six problems.
~ After each problem, you get one step closer to the crown!
~ At the end of each level there will be a test and all questions must be answered right in order to move on to the next level.

Good Luck!
